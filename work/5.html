⭐ Star Gazing with Dash and Plotly

3D visualization of stars in the universe, with Dash and Plotly

3D Visualzation of Stars ⭐ in Our Universe | Mar - May, 2023

The visualization randomly samples 10k stars in our universe and plot them in 3D with their Cartesian coordinates, absoulte magnitude (radius) and temperature (color). You can play around with the visualization by interacting with the graph like zooming in or out, or set filters on the right panel. This visualization is powered by Plotly and Dash.

(https://stargazingnight.herokuapp.com/) | Github
